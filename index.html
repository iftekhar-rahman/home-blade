ABOUT AGAVE AZUL

Situated on pristine wooded terrain in Central Mexico’s high desert landscape, Agave Azul is a short 15 minutes away from the bustling center of San Miguel de Allende, named the “Best City in the World” for three years in a row by Conde Nast Traveler and Travel & Leisure magazines.

The Agave Azul experience is a truly congenial one. If dining on property, guests are served together family-style, often making lifelong friends with table mates from around the world. Whether solo or in a group, our guests are given the opportunity to mix, mingle and share experiences during their stay.

TOURS & ACTIVITIES

Whatever your goals may be for your visit, the Agave Azul family is here to help deliver your best experience. We specialize in guided tours of historic San Miguel as well as other “Pueblos Magicos” in the region.

We can arrange cooking classes, shopping tours, hot air balloon rides, art studio visits and more. We work in advance of your stay to create an itinerary informed by your preferences.
